The App does not directly collect or store personal information on its own servers. However, the following third-party services may process certain information to provide their functionality.

Service Purpose
Google AdMob Advertising and ad performance measurement
Google Play Billing In-app purchase processing

2. Information That May Be Processed

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Displaying advertisements

4. Calculator Data

All calculations are performed entirely on the user's device. Mathematical expressions and calculation results are never transmitted to external servers.

5. Permissions

The App may use the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET

The App does not request access to contacts, location, photos, or other sensitive personal information.
